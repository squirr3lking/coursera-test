Chicken

Rhode Island Red didn't get his name from his rosy-hued comb and wattle - he got it from all his years in the pits. Only the best birds can fight Ol' Red: Arnie the Arucana, Lenny "the Gamecock" Leghorn, and Stumpy Bantam - the all-Chickanian lightweight Champion. All of them have fallen to Red. Rest assured, your dinner plate is graced with the cream of the crop.

Beef

Waygu? Kobe? No way. If you want to eat hipster cows, I hear California has a great selection. Our cows don't need massages or craft beer and they sure as hell don't brush their hides in public. Our beef comes from hard-working, salt-of-the-earth cattle that put in 12-hour days and are thankful for it. Our cows wear leather not because it's trendy, but because it's a working cow's material - tough enough for any situation.
